Dufourspitze/Punta Dufour 4634 m. Dufourspitze/Punta Dufour belongs to the Monte Rosa massif and is the third highest mountain of the Alps, after Mont Blanc 4810 m and Monte Bianco di Courmayeur 4748 m.
